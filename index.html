About

Working underneath the model

I'm a Computer Science student at Sardar Patel Institute of Technology, Mumbai, working across computer vision, LLM systems, and applied research. Most of what interests me lives one layer below the API — sparse sampling strategies, attention geometry, agent orchestration, and the exact place a system quietly breaks under load.

Right now I split my time between two things: Zenso, a video engagement analytics platform built on sparse-frame video understanding, and Sentinel, a multi-agent harness for red-teaming LLMs across text, image, and video delivery.
